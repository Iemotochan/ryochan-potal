Characters

RYO-CHAN

Bloodline: Descendant of White Cloud, Miniature Schnauzer
Awakening Site: Ancient Shrine on a Full Moon Night
Special Abilities: Ki Visualization, Malice Detection, Contract Analysis
A guardian dog from a special bloodline dating back to 1945. Awakened to the power of "Ki" on a full moon night, gaining the ability to detect malicious smart contracts. Clad in small warrior armor, she is a destined warrior protecting the cryptocurrency world with the belief of "bark with courage, bite with honor."

The Shrine's Elder Keeper

Form of Existence: Spirit appearing on full moon nights
Relationship: Sakura's grandfather, RYO-CHAN's mentor
Knowledge: Ancient Ki, Katakamuna, Traditional Wisdom
The last guardian who knows the secrets of "Ki" passed down in Japan since ancient times. Foresees the fusion of the power of true kanji lost after the war with modern technology. A mysterious being who grants RYO-CHAN her mission and guides her to become a bridge between tradition and innovation. The light emanating from his staff shows the true path to lost souls.

SAKURA

Occupation: Blockchain Researcher
Development: RYO SCAN System, AI Integration Technology
Mission: Science & Ki Fusion, Anti-Fraud Technology
Granddaughter of the shrine's elder keeper and a brilliant technologist. Fuses RYO-CHAN's ability to sense "Ki" with scientific technology to develop the innovative RYO SCAN system. A crucial figure bridging traditional wisdom with cutting-edge technology, protecting the safety of the cryptocurrency world.

The Dark Organizations

Composition: TSUKI, DDWO, Independent Hackers
Purpose: Ki Dominance, Mental Control, Information Manipulation
Methods: Complex Fraud, System Infiltration, Psychological Manipulation
Dark forces led by the secret organization TSUKI, continuing since the post-war era. They seek to weaken Japan's "Ki" and abuse digital technology to dominate people. Not simple evil, but intelligent enemies with complex motivations and advanced technology. The existence that most fears RYO-CHAN's abilities.

The Mastermind

Identity: ████████ (Classified Information)
Plan: New World Order, Complete Control System
Related: 70-year Long-term Strategy
The mysterious figure at the center of all conspiracies. The true mastermind who has intentionally weakened Japan's spirituality from the post-war reconstruction period to the present. When their true identity is revealed, RYO-CHAN and her companions will face their greatest trial... What is their true purpose?
Episode 01

Awakening

On a night of the full moon, a small dog wandered into an ancient shrine. That was the beginning of everything. What awaited RYO-CHAN, who awakened enveloped in mystical light, was an unimaginable story of destiny. The prophecy spoken by an ancient sage, the awakening of hidden powers, and a mission to transform the world... However, shadows lurk behind it all. Will RYO-CHAN be able to accept the destiny thrust upon her?

Episode 02

Distortion

A dark organization lurking in the Tokyo nights. Using advanced technology, they were plotting something in the depths of the digital world. Meanwhile, RYO-CHAN's awakening created ripples beyond expectations, and inexplicable phenomena began occurring everywhere. As ancient powers and cutting-edge technology intersect, the line between good and evil becomes blurred. Who is the true enemy? And can RYO-CHAN stand against the approaching crisis?
