You can get RYO Coins through our licensed exchange partner in Japan, at RYO ATMs, or by receiving them from friends who already have RYO! The LIFE Wallet app makes it super easy to start!

Yes! RYO uses super secure blockchain technology to keep your money safe. Plus, it's being developed with regulatory compliance in mind, especially in Japan where cryptocurrency rules are clear!
